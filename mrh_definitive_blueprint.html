🔒 Concurrency Guardrails (Booking)
Order ثابت في كل booking transaction:
1 → pg_advisory_xact_lock(hashtext('tutor:' + id))
2 → pessimistic_write على StudentProfile
3 → Timezone convert → Availability check → 400
4 → Range overlap (existingStart < newEnd AND existingEnd > newStart) → 409
5 → Balance check → 400
6 → Deduct + Save PENDING
